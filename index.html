About

Building software with purpose.

I've always believed software should do more than just work. It should leave people better than they were before they used it. Whether I'm creating games, mobile apps or websites, my goal is to combine creativity with problem solving to build experiences that are enjoyable, memorable and genuinely useful.

At HEART/Amber Coding Academy, I completed beginner-level web projects that helped me learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and core programming habits. At Vector Technology Institute, I built a mobile app with Java and XML layouts and designed a hospital database system that strengthened my SQL and data modeling skills.

My long-term vision is to build games that entertain while also helping people learn, improve their confidence and develop real-world skills. I use AI as a development assistant to prototype faster, debug issues, and improve my workflow while still leading the design, testing, and creative direction of each project.

I love creating games from the ground up, whether that means Scratch, Unity, Android, or browser-based projects. I enjoy designing systems, testing ideas, refining mechanics, and turning small prototypes into more polished software experiences.

Sports have always been a big part of my life, from football and basketball to badminton, swimming, and track and field. Coding has become one of my favorite everyday hobbies because it lets me turn ideas into something people can actually use or play.

My strongest areas are UI/UX thinking, HTML, CSS, Unity and C#. I am building working knowledge in JavaScript, Python, MySQL, Kotlin and Android Studio while continuing to grow through regular projects, testing and debugging.
